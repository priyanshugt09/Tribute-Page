Biography

Shah Rukh Khan, often called the "King of Bollywood", is one of the most iconic and influential actors in Indian cinema. He was born on November 2, 1965, in New Delhi, India, and his journey from humble beginnings to superstardom is truly inspiring. Shah Rukh Khan has acted in over 80 films, spanning genres from romantic dramas to action thrillers. His early hits like "Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge" (1995) and "Kabhi Khushi Kabhie Gham" (2001) cemented his status as a romantic hero. Over time, he diversified his roles, portraying complex characters in movies like "My Name is Khan" (2010) and "Chak De! India" (2007), which showcased his acting range and his ability to deliver meaningful social messages. Known for his unmatched charisma, Shah Rukh's screen presence has been a major factor in his international appeal, making him one of the most recognized Indian actors globally.
